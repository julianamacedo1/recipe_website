Spinach Dip

(short description)

(pictures)

Ingredients

20 ounces frozen spinach
8 ounces cream cheese
1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
1/4 cup mayo
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 tablespoon parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon seasoned breadcrumbs

Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Microwave frozen spinach (20 ounces) until thawed.
3. Squeeze out excess water from spinach.
4. Microwave cream cheese (8 ounces) until soft.
5. Add thawed spinach and softened cream cheese to a large bowl and mix until combined.
6. Add plain greek yogurt (1/4 cup), mayo (1/4 cup), parmesan cheese (1/4 cup), red pepper flakes (1 teaspoon), salt (1/4 teaspoon), pepper (1/4 teaspoon), garlic powder (1 teaspoon), and onion powder (1 teaspoon) to spinach and cream cheese mixture and mix until combined.
7. Add dip mixture to baking dish.
8. Sprinkle parmesan cheese (1 tablespoon) and seasoned bread crumbs (1 tablespoon) on top.
9. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes or until golden brown on top.
10. Dip.
